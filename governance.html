Clinical Governance

Governance &
Platform Standards

The Mohsin Haematology Academy is committed to transparent, responsible, and evidence-aligned clinical education. This page sets out the governance framework that underpins everything published on this platform.

Important: All content on this platform is intended for educational purposes only. It does not constitute direct clinical advice and must not be used as a substitute for individual patient assessment, specialist consultation, or current national guidelines.

Framework
How this platform operates

Scope

This platform provides educational decision-support content for haematology trainees and clinicians. It is designed to complement — not replace — formal training, local protocols, and national guidance.

  • Structured educational content and clinical frameworks
  • Digital decision-support tools for point-of-care learning
  • Guideline summaries aligned to NICE, BSH, and ELN standards

Limitations

Users must be aware of the following limitations when using this platform:

  • Content reflects national guidelines and may not align with local trust protocols
  • Clinical guidelines evolve — always verify against the most current published version
  • Individual patient circumstances always require clinical judgement

Data & Privacy

This platform is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • No patient data is collected, stored, or processed
  • No user accounts or login credentials are required
  • All tools operate entirely within the user's browser — no data leaves the device
  • Analytics data (visit counts) is collected in aggregate only via Cloudflare

Evidence Standards

All clinical content is anchored to recognised national and international guidance:

  • NICE guidelines (with guideline number and publication date)
  • British Society for Haematology (BSH) standards
  • European LeukemiaNet (ELN) recommendations where applicable
  • Peer-reviewed literature for tool development and validation

Content Review & Versioning

All tools and resources carry a version number and last-updated date. Content is reviewed when relevant guidelines are updated or when clinical practice evolves significantly. Users are encouraged to check the version date and cross-reference with the source guideline before applying content in practice.

Reporting a Concern

If you identify a clinical inaccuracy, an outdated guideline reference, or a potential patient safety concern in any content on this platform, please contact us directly via the Contact page. All reports are reviewed promptly and content is corrected or removed where necessary.

Independent platform notice: The Mohsin Haematology Academy is an independent educational initiative. Content reflects the educational perspective of the author and does not represent the views, policies, or clinical protocols of any NHS Trust, employer, Royal College, or professional body. Clinical guidance and local protocols vary between institutions — always refer to your local policies and the most current national guidelines when making clinical decisions.
