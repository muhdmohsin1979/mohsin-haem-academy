HaemCalc

HaemCalc is a structured haematology calculator designed to support interpretation of laboratory parameters and assist clinical reasoning in defined scenarios.

It has been developed with a focus on clarity, usability, and practical application in real-world clinical settings.

Open HaemCalc

Purpose

HaemCalc is intended to provide structured support for interpreting haematological parameters and identifying clinically relevant patterns. It is designed to complement clinical thinking rather than replace it.

Intended users

This tool is primarily intended for haematology trainees, internal medicine trainees, and clinicians managing haematology patients in routine clinical practice.

Important information

This tool is provided for educational purposes only. Outputs must always be interpreted in the full clinical context and should not be used in isolation to guide patient management.

No patient-identifiable data should be entered into this tool.
